--- layout: layouts/pages/bikes/refurbs.njk bannerImage: src/static/images/banner-refurb.jpg title: Refurbs tags: - main - refurbs eleventyNavigation: parent: Bikes key: Refurbs title: Refurbs order: 4 --- _**Bikes for the community**_ Our community up-cycle programme gives old bikes a new life with the goal to get more people using bikes for everyday travel in Aberfeldy. From Grandtully to Kenmore, the Upper Tay Valley is a great place to use a bike for trips to the shops, visiting friends, getting outdoors, and more. And cycling helps Aberfeldy become [Scotland's Healthiest Town](https://www.healthiesttown.org/). Our programme has 3 steps:
  • Donate 'em
    Donate your old/unused bikes - even if they're broken we can use many of the parts
  • Fix 'em
    We'll do any necessary maintenance/fixes and ensure they're safe to ride (at no charge)
  • Ride 'em
    The bikes will be donated or sold at minimal cost for use in the community
