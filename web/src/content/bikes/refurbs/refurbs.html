From Grandtully to Kenmore, the Upper Tay Valley is a great place to use a bike for trips to the shops, visiting friends, getting outdoors, and more. And cycling helps Aberfeldy become [Scotland's Healthiest Town](https://www.healthiesttown.org/). Our programme has 3 steps:
  • Donate 'em
    Donate your old/unused bikes - even if they're broken we can use many of the parts
  • Fix 'em
    We'll do any necessary maintenance/fixes and ensure they're safe to ride (at no charge)
  • Ride 'em
    The bikes will be donated or sold at minimal cost for use in the community

Looking for a bike?

We usually have a selection of refurbed bikes available for purchase, or short-term use. Pop into the workshop (or just peer through window) to view our current offering. Looking to upgrade your kids bike to a larger size? We'll gladly accept a swap.
