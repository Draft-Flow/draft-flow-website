--- layout: layouts/page.njk pageID: staticPages.about tags: - secondary eleventyNavigation: key: About title: About order: 1 --- Draft & Flow started as a home-based bike mechanic service out of Brett DeWoody's kitchen in the fall of 2022. It soon became clear there was a big demand for bike repair in Aberfeldy. As a result Draft & Flow is now changing course. We're joining forces with a few other cycle-minded residents, converting to a Community Interest Company, and setting up a community-centred cycle workshop on Aberfeldy's town square. The shop will offer: - Tools for DIY repairs - Bike repair servicing - Maintenance courses - Parts, accessories and bike sales - Bike hire - Riding info We hope to open our doors in early 2024.
