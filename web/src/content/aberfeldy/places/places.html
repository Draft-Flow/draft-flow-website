But in a world of it's own, with a variety of riding to suit nearly everyone. With the Cairngorm National Park to the east, and Loch Lomond & The Trossachs to the west, Aberfeldy sits at the southern end of the highlands with access to a wide range of terrain and activities. If you're looking for places to ride, check our [recommended Routes](/local-routes/).

Things to Do

  • The Birks Cinema
    A modern cinema, cafe and bar located in a 1930s art deco building
  • Beyond Adventure
    A leader in paddlesports instruction, expeditions and guiding
  • Wee Adventures
    Outdoor adventures perfect for kids and families
  • Scottish Crannog Centre
    Museum situated on the banks of Loch Tay
  • Highland Safaris
    Cafe, red deer centre, guided highland safaries, and more
  • Taymouth Marina
    Suana, hydrotherapy pool, watersports, lodging, and food, with a superb view

Places to Eat & Drink

  • Glen Lyon Coffee Roasters
    Roastery and coffee hangout, perfect for a pre-ride boost
  • Cow & Parrot
    Outstanding coffee and delicious muffins
  • Ailean Chraggan
    Hotel and restaurant with great views just outside of Aberfeldy
  • The Tay Cafe
    Takeaway sandwiches, salads, hot rolls, and drinks
  • Aberfeldy Pubs
    Several pubs serving hearty food and drinks

Other Bikes Shops

  • Progression Bikes
    Located in Dunkeld, they offer a range of hire bikes, gear and servicing
  • Escape Route
    Located in Pitlochry, they offer a range of bike services and gear
  • Braw Bikes
    BrawBikes is a dedicated bicycle maintenance service located in Murthly, Perthshire.

People to Ride With

  • Draft & Flow Social Rides
    Family-friendly rides at party pace - ride with the locals!
